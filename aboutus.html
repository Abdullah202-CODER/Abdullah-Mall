About Us

Our Story

Since our grand opening in 2024, Abdullah Mall has been a hub of excitement and innovation,
redefining the shopping experience for Faisalabad. We believe that a mall is more than just a place
to shop; it's a community space where people come together to create memories, celebrate special
moments, and enjoy life’s pleasures.

What We Offer

At Abdullah Mall, we pride ourselves on offering an extensive selection of high-end fashion brands,
trendy boutiques, and essential retail stores. Whether you’re looking for the latest in fashion,
electronics, home decor, or beauty products, you’ll find everything you need under one roof.

But shopping is just the beginning. Our mall is also home to a variety of gourmet restaurants, casual
dining spots, and cafes, perfect for a quick bite or a leisurely meal. And when it comes to
entertainment, we’ve got you covered with state-of-the-art cinemas, family-friendly activities, and
regular events that keep the energy alive throughout the year.

Join Us

We invite you to explore, discover, and enjoy all that Abdullah Mall has to offer. Whether you’re here
to shop, dine, or simply spend time with loved ones, we’re committed to making every visit special.

Thank you for choosing Abdullah Mall. We look forward to welcoming you again and again!
